My Synopsis

Criminal Mastermind Artemis Fowl has new competition with Minerva Paradizo as they race to reach a lost colony of demons that's been strangely separated from the rest of The People. With the help of Holly, Foaly, Mulch, and a new friend, Artemis and Butler launch a plan to sabotage their competition. Through various endeavors, Artemis, Holly Short, and their newfound friend travel through space and time and end up stuck with a bomb and one too many aggressive residents of this lost colony. Artemis has to find a way back with nothing but a bomb, Holly and their friend while their time left seemingly ticks away faster and faster.


Main Characters

Artemis Fowl

Artemis is much more than just a kid genius, after all, he managed to reteach himself everything he learned about the fairy people over a couple of years in less than 24 hours after getting his mind wiped. Now he has to figure out what's up with Leon Abbot and his pride before everything comes loose and destroys Earth.

Leon Abbot

A megalomaniac of a Demon with some of the best combat skills of the Demons, Abbot is the cause of many problems Artemis is presented within this adventure. Abbot sees Artemis, Holly and their new friend as a threat to him and his endeavors so goes to such unnecessary lengths to get rid of them that he almost destroys the Earth.

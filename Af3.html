My Synopsis

13-year-old criminal mastermind Artemis Fowl attempts to scam an immense amount of money from the powerful and extremely dangerous businessman Jon Spiro. Artemis ends up getting sucked into much more than he opted for when he tries to exploit Jon Spiro using some stolen fairy technology.


Main Characters

Artemis Fowl

Although Artemis is pretty intelligent and quick to think on his feet, his ego might be biting off more than he can handle with Jon Sprio

Butler

Sworn to protect Artemis over everything else, Butler is the only person, fairy or not, to fight a troll on relatively equal terms in hand-to-hand combat. Now with Artemis trying to deceive the all-mighty Jon Spiro, Butler's hands are full attempting to keep Artemis safe from everything Jon Spiro throws their way.

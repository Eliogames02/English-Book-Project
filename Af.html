My Synopsis

A twelve-year-old criminal mastermind named Artemis Fowl begins to delve too far into the secrets of a hidden people. These technologically advanced fairies are extraordinarily dangerous. He kidnaps Holly Short and holds her for ransom. With his bodyguard Butler by his side, Artemis must find a resolution unless war breaks out between Humanity and The People.


Main Characters

Artemis Fowl

Artemis is an extremely cunning kid. So much so that the extremely advanced Fairy code took him only 14 hours to completely decode. Artemis is also quite a greedy person, as per his efforts to restore his family legacy and wealth by putting Holly Short up for ransom.

Holly Short

Captain Holly Short works for the LEP, which is a police force for The People. She's sent on field missions to deal with fairy-related issues above ground, which is a cause of how she got captured by the infamous Artemis Fowl.
